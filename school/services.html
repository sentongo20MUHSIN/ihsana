Our Programs

Quran Studies,Islamic Jurisprudence & Character Education

Comprehensive teaching of Quranic recitation and memorization. Understanding the meanings and teachings of the Quran. Learning about Islamic laws and practices. Applying Islamic principles in daily life. & Focus on moral and ethical development. Instilling values such as honesty, compassion, and respect.

Sports Program

At Ihsaan Junior School, we believe in the importance of physical education and sports as a vital part of our holistic educational approach. Our sports program for primary pupils is designed to promote physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and a love for healthy competition.

Secular Programs

Our secular programs are integrated with our holistic educational approach, ensuring that students receive a balanced education that prepares them for both academic success and personal growth. At Ihsaan Junior School, we strive to nurture well-rounded individuals who are equipped to contribute positively to society.
