The History Of Ihsaan Junior School

In 2017, the visionary director, Hajj Muzamil Lubega, laid the foundation for Ihsaan Baby School with a small group of 24 pupils. With a commitment to providing quality education, the school officially opened its doors in 2019, welcoming 109 eager learners. The subsequent year, 2020, witnessed remarkable growth, with the pupil count soaring to 196. Despite the global challenges posed by the Covid-19 epidemic, which led to a temporary closure in 2020 and 2021, the school triumphantly resumed teaching in 2022 with an increased enrollment of 226 pupils.
