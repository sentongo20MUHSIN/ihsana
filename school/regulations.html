Rules and Regulations

  1. Home
  2. Regulations

The following shall be rules and regulations for every pupil in school. These rules and regulations shall be amended from the time as need arises with or without the pupil's consent.

  • These rules and regulations shall be duplicated and handed to each pupil on entering the school where they will sign print them and once copy is filed in his or her personal life.
  • Each pupil shall be required to be in position of the rules and regulations and urged to study them effectively.
  • He / She shall attend classes and be punctual for all routine duties and shall be set by the school authority.
  • He / She shall always put on decently and in the prescribed school uniform which he/she shall obtain from the school.
  • He / She shall always appear smart in shoes, with well trimmed, combed or well plaited hair.
  • He / She shall be medically and physically sound and the school always carries out medical examination and pupils found unfit or sick shall be asked to go for treatment. The school may provide some treatment and the costs shall be incurred by the user.
  • He / She shall devote most of his or her time on his or her studies including co-curricular activities as shall be organised by the school authority.
  • He / She shall respect all school property and he/she shall always respect his/her friend's property and shall respect his/her own personal property.
  • He / She shall always use official language, English, while at school unless allowed by the teachers to use other languages.
  • He / She shall always respect all staff members and visitors of the school.
  • He / She shall not bring visitors unknown to the school into the dormitories or school compound and all visitors shall be introduced to the school administrators.
  • He / She shall not use abusive language, mine, gestures or any oter abusive signs to the staff, fellow pupils, visitors or any other person.
  • He / She shall always be kind to his or her fellow pupils, staff members, animals and plants all the times while at the school and home.
  • He / She shall always be kind to his or her religion and that of his/her fellow pupils and shall observe days of prayer and his/her religious demands.
  • He / She shall not steal, destroy and damage or hide school property or that of his or her school mate.
Ihsaan Junior School

Our secular programs are integrated with our holistic educational approach, ensuring that students receive a balanced education that prepares them for both academic success and personal growth. At Ihsaan Junior School, we strive to nurture well-rounded individuals who are equipped to contribute positively to society.
